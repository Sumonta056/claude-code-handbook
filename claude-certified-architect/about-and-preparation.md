# About & Preparation

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (17).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Claude Certified Architect (CCA) is a certification program designed to validate the skills and knowledge of professionals in the field of AI and machine learning, specifically focusing on the Claude AI platform. The certification aims to ensure that individuals possess the necessary expertise to design, implement, and manage AI solutions using Claude.
